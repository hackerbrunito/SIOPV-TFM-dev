## 1. PURPOSE

Stage 3.5 is a pre-processing step before Stage 4. Its job is to extract key findings from the Stage 1, Stage 2, and Stage 3 final audit reports, and compile them into one structured input brief that the Stage 4 orchestrator will read.

Stage 4 cannot rely on the raw final reports directly because they are too large to feed into multiple agents without exceeding context limits. Stage 3.5 solves this by having dedicated extraction agents produce compact, structured delta documents, which the aggregator then merges into one final brief.

## 3. EXECUTION FLOW

### Phase 0 — Orchestrator writes plan
1. team-lead creates team `siopv-stage3.5`
2. team-lead spawns orchestrator with model: sonnet
3. Orchestrator reads its instructions, writes its plan to file (see Section 7)
4. Orchestrator sends plan to team-lead via SendMessage
5. **HUMAN CHECKPOINT 0** — human approves plan before any agent is spawned

### Round 1 — Parallel extraction (3 agents)
6. team-lead spawns extractor-stage1, extractor-stage2, extractor-stage3 simultaneously
7. All 3 run in parallel — no file write conflicts
8. Each extractor saves its report and sends SendMessage to orchestrator confirming completion
9. Orchestrator waits for all 3 confirmations, then sends summary to team-lead
10. **HUMAN CHECKPOINT 1** — human reviews extractor outputs, approves before aggregator runs

### Round 2 — Aggregation (1 agent)
11. team-lead spawns aggregator
12. Aggregator reads all 3 extractor reports, merges them, saves final brief
13. Aggregator sends SendMessage to orchestrator confirming completion
14. Orchestrator sends final brief path to team-lead
15. **HUMAN CHECKPOINT 2** — human reviews final brief, approves
16. Orchestrator stops and waits. team-lead shuts down team.

## Part 4: Cross-cutting Recommendations
Any observation across all three extractor reports that applies to more than
one section or that the aggregator identifies as a cross-cutting pattern.
